Intelligent driving system for vehicles

View of the installation inside the tractor cab

Product: The software and hardware for autonomous self-plowing tractor.(2021)

The software can distinguish patterns and objects for navigation on the farm field. Recognition is carried out by video cameras and laser sensors. The data is processed by the program and the signal is transmitted to the control levers for the control of the tractor. The product was tested on real tractor.
